About Us

We are a company committed to sustainability and sports. We have created an application that connects volunteers with sporting events, allowing them to participate and collaborate to make each event a more ecological, responsible, and supportive experience.

Our goal is to promote volunteering in the sports sector, encouraging teamwork, respect for the environment, and social awareness in every activity.

Our History

Green Volunteering was born in 2025 thanks to a group of athletes and environmental activists who shared a common vision: transforming sporting events into drivers of positive change for our planet. What began as a small local initiative in Bilbao has now become a network of more than 5,000 active volunteers across Spain.

Every year we collaborate in more than 200 sporting events, from marathons and triathlons to surfing and mountain biking competitions, ensuring that each event leaves a positive impact on the environment and the local community.
