About us

We are a company committed to sustainability and sports. We have created an application that connects volunteers with sports events, allowing them to participate and collaborate to make every event a more ecological, responsible, and supportive experience.

Our goal is to promote volunteering in the sports field, fostering teamwork, respect for the environment, and social awareness in every activity.
